How does the constitution work to reach the goals?



  1. To Form a perfect union- Forming a more perfect Union means building a country that can use the advantages of the strength the states gained from working together.

  2. Establish justice- In the 14th amendment it talks about due processes and it also states that "in the criminal justice system, you must protect and uphold an individual's rights and liberties, which includes; fair respect, and fair ethnical treatment etc." The article that goes with this amendment is Article III Section 1 "The judicial Power of the United States, shall be vested in one supreme Court, and in such inferior Courts as the Congress may from time to time ordain and establish. The Judges, both of the supreme and inferior Courts, shall hold their Offices during good Behavior, and shall, at stated Times, receive for their Services, a Compensation, which shall not be diminished during their Continuance in Office

  3. Ensure domestic tranquility- preserve civil peace so that everyone could live their lives without fear of social strife.

  4. Provide for the common defense- It means to protect the United States, its citizens and its property. The amendment that goes with this is the 10th. It gives powers to the states and reserved to the states. The article that goes with this is Article IV. ''The United States shall guarantee to every State in this Union a Republican Form of Government and shall protect each of them against Invasion; and on Application of the Legislature, or of the Executive (when the Legislature cannot be convened) against domestic Violence. ''

  5. Promote the general welfare- The concern of the government for our health, peace, and safety of us the citizens. Providing for the welfare of the general public is one of the basic goals of the government. The preamble in the U.S. Congress is charged by the Constitution with providing for the general welfare of the country's citizens. This means improving transportation, improving agriculture and industry, protecting health and the environment, and looking for ways to solve social and economic problems.



    Article I, Section 8, Clause 1: The Congress shall have Power To lay and collect Taxes, Duties, Imposts and Excises, to pay the Debts and provide for the common defense and general Welfare of the United States; but all Duties, Imposts and Excises shall be uniform throughout the United States;



  6. Secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our prosperity- the rights and freedom of every citizen. The meaning or purpose of this phrase in the preamble to the US constitution is to ensure that the rights of the people for liberty are duly preserved for themselves and the sake of posterity.

We the people of the United States, in order to form a more perfect union, establish justice, ensure domestic tranquility, provide for the common defense, promote the general welfare, and secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the United States of America.
