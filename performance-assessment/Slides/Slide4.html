The Fifth Amendment



  • The fifth amendment helps meet the goal of Establishing Justice. An example of this would be the protection that is granted to the witnesses, the protection against self incrimination.

  • An example of how this would be helpful is in a court case, if the witness was also breaking a crime during the time of testimony, the witness can please the fifth. What this will do is let the witness testify without incriminating themselves.
