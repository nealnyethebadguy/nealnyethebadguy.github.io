Concept: Homestead

A simple thing to help issues in the current wealth distribution system

Homestead contains-

  • Solar Panel roof
  • Garden Lot
  • Free Water (2 years)
  • Internet (shared with other units, 1GBPS bus)
  • Computer unit, integrated with homestead

Proposition

In order to modernize rural areas and to help solve issues of poverty and debt, I propose a solution, give people in need a one time offer, a simple homestead to help get them off their feet and become a more functioning member in society. This project aims to lessen the cost the government spends on people that may need help, while still increasing their quality of life.

Dimensions/design goals

While there are no set dimensions, there are a set of loose design goals

  • Each unit should be completely modular, making it possible to combine more than one unit to make a family house.
  • The vast majority of space should be taken care of vertically, in height, rather than horizontally to make the units light in land size.
  • The units should be fairly modern, with equipment to help monitor electricity use, water use, and usage of other equipments
  • The units should provide a relatively good quality of life, and should be pleasant to live in.
  • The units should be cheap, under 50k Maximum budget per unit.
